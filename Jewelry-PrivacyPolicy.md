While we strive to provide accurate information, Jewelry Identifier's identification feature is not infallible. We do not guarantee the accuracy of identifications, and users should not rely solely on our app for determining the authenticity or value of any jewelry. Always consult with certified experts for professional appraisals.

Please be aware that this data transmission is subject to OpenAI's privacy practices as well as ours. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy policy for more information on how they handle data.
